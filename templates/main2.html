Inspiration for this project

The purpose of this project, “Say My Name” is to show the correlation between popular baby names by year and popular movie character’s, with a focus on the top 10. The goal is to provide an interactive website that will display and separate data by:

  • Name
  • Year
  • Gender
  • Count( This selection will show the number of baby names by state from most popular to most common.)

    • The data sources were retrieved from two datasets: Baby Names from Social Security Card Applications – National Level Data with years from 1879-2017
